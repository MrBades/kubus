About Kubus Engineering

Our history, values, and why we are your ideal partner.

Our Journey

Kubus Engineering is an indigenously owned Limited Liability Company duly registered on 1st November 2019, with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and other appropriate relevant authorities in Nigeria. With our head office in Enugu and branch offices in Lagos and Abuja, we ensure wide coverage and seamless service delivery across Africa.

We are a team of seasoned Software Engineers and Telecommunication experts, collaborating with international partners from China, India, and the USA to deliver first-class, cutting-edge tech services. We are known for world-class practices that guarantee an astonishing 99.99% service availability, complemented by a proactive preventive maintenance strategy.
