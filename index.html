About Kubus Engineering

Our history, values, and why we are your ideal partner.

Our Journey

Kubus Engineering is an indigenously owned Limited Liability Company duly registered on 1st November 2019, with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and other appropriate relevant authorities in Nigeria. With our head office in Enugu and branch offices in Lagos and Abuja, we ensure wide coverage and seamless service delivery across Africa.

We are a team of seasoned Software Engineers and Telecommunication experts, collaborating with international partners from China, India, and the USA to deliver first-class, cutting-edge tech services. We are known for world-class practices that guarantee an astonishing 99.99% service availability, complemented by a proactive preventive maintenance strategy.

      Core Service Deep Dive

      Network Infrastructure

      Our Network Infrastructure services form the bedrock of modern digital connectivity. We design, build, and maintain robust, high-performance networks that serve as the central nervous system for businesses, schools, and communities. From urban centers to the most remote locations, we deliver reliable connectivity that empowers communication, collaboration, and growth.

      Key Offerings in this Service:

      • Network Design & Installation: We conduct thorough site surveys and needs analysis to architect bespoke network solutions. This includes planning and deploying high-capacity microwave links for point-to-point connections, resilient fiber optic backbones, and wide-coverage radio networks.
      • Starlink & Satellite Internet Setup: As certified installers, we bridge the digital divide by deploying Starlink and traditional VSAT solutions, bringing high-speed, low-latency internet to areas where terrestrial infrastructure is unavailable. Our service includes installation, activation, and ongoing performance management.
      • LAN/WAN Integration: We create seamless and secure office environments through expert Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN) integration. This encompasses structured cabling, router and switch configuration, and integrating disparate systems into a single, cohesive network.
      • Data Cabling (Cat6/Cat7): The physical layer is critical. We provide professional installation, termination, and Fluke testing certification for Cat6/Cat7 copper cabling, ensuring your network's physical integrity meets the highest industry standards for speed and reliability.

      Power & Energy Solutions

      In an environment where consistent power is a challenge, our Energy Solutions are designed for maximum resilience and efficiency. We specialize in creating hybrid power systems that guarantee uninterrupted operations for critical infrastructure, reducing reliance on unstable grids and costly fossil fuels. Our solutions are sustainable, reliable, and built to last.

      Key Offerings in this Service:

      • Solar Power Installation: We design and deploy commercial-grade solar power systems tailored to your specific load requirements. Our service includes panel installation, inverter configuration, and integration with existing power sources to provide clean, renewable energy and significantly reduce operational costs.
      • Battery Backup Systems: We implement high-capacity battery banks using advanced lithium-ion or deep-cycle technologies. These systems store energy from solar or the grid, providing hours or even days of autonomous power during outages, ensuring 100% uptime for your critical systems.
      • Inverter Installation & Maintenance: The inverter is the heart of any backup system. We install and maintain pure sine wave inverters that provide stable, clean power, safe for sensitive electronic equipment. Our proactive maintenance ensures your system is always ready when you need it most.
      • Grid Connection & Optimization: For sites with access to the national grid, we manage the entire connection process. This includes installing Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) for seamless failover, metering, and performing power load balancing to prevent overloads and optimize energy consumption.

      Cybersecurity Solutions

      As connectivity grows, so do the threats. Our multi-layered Cybersecurity Solutions are designed to protect your digital assets from the inside out. We move beyond basic antivirus, implementing a proactive, defense-in-depth strategy that identifies, prevents, and responds to sophisticated cyber threats, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of your data.

      Key Offerings in this Service:

      • Firewall Installation & Management: We are experts in deploying and managing industry-leading firewalls, including Cisco IOS, ASA, and Checkpoint. We configure access control lists (ACLs), intrusion prevention systems (IPS), and VPNs to create a hardened perimeter against unauthorized access.
      • Endpoint Security: Your devices are the frontline. We deploy advanced Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) solutions that go beyond traditional antivirus, using AI and behavioral analysis to detect and block malware, ransomware, and zero-day exploits in real-time.
      • Cybersecurity Software Licensing: Navigating the world of security software can be complex. We act as your trusted advisor and reseller, providing and managing licenses for best-in-class security tools, ensuring you get the right protection at the best value.
      • Security Protocols & Audits: We help you build a culture of security by implementing robust protocols for data handling, access control, and incident response. We conduct regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify and remediate weaknesses before they can be exploited.
